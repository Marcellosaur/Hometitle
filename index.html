About uS

Home Title Company is a full service agency that offers the entire spectrum of real estate title and closing services. Once the purchase agreement is signed, Home Title Company will begin the process of paperwork to make sure that all parties to the transaction are identified; that there are no liens on the property and that the title is clear to transfer to the new owners. Lenders also work directly with Home Title Company during the loan application and underwriting process. Home Title Company will also handle the payoffs with the seller’s mortgage company; in addition to collecting for HOA dues, property tax proration’s and other fees that pertain to selling a house. We also assist the real estate agents if any legal matters arise during the closing process. We are experienced in all types of real estate transactions which includes both residential and commercial property. We are a Stewart Title Agency Partner.

Title Insurance Must Knows

Why do I need Title Insurance?

Buying a piece of real estate will more than likely be the largest purchase of your lifetime. In order to protect your interest, title insurance is available to protect this investment against any property loss or damage an owner may experience because of liens, encumbrances or defects in the title of the property. A defect in title can be things such as another person claiming an ownership interest, improperly recorded documents, fraud, forgery, liens, encroachments, easements, and other items that may create a legal issue with the title of the property.

Who Needs Title Insurance?

Lenders will always require the purchase of title insurance to cover their investment in the loan they provide to purchases. Purchasers have the option to add owner's title insurance at the time of closing for a small additional fee.
